8) Tap on package "Core Library".

9) Tap "Install" and then "Confirm".

 

10) Tap "Restart SpringBoard". Your device will be respringed.

11) Find and tap on Pumpic icon.

12) Check the "Accept" box and tap "Next".

 

13) Now the app will ask you to enter a PIN code, which you can get in the next step of these Guidelines.

Now click "Next" to proceed to the next step and complete the installation.
