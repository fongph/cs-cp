1. Set up the Android Device

It's Important

The device must have at least 60% of battery life left for rooting purposes.

  1. Put the device into the "USB Debugging" mode by taping on Settings – Developer options (or Settings – Application – Development – USB Debugging *on older versions of Android OS).
