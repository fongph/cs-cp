1. How to enable backup on your target device.

    Take the target Apple iOS device. Then go to Settings > iCloud > Backup. If iCloud Backup is off, please turn it ON (as shown below).

    NOTE: for iOS 6-8 path is Settings > iCloud > Storage & Backup

      

  2. How to back up your target device manually.

    Take the target Apple iOS device. Then go to Settings > iCloud > Backup and tap on "Back up now". Wait till backup process is finished. This may take several minutes.

     

  3. How to back up your target device automatically

    If backup is enabled and the following requirements are satisfied, your device will back up to iCloud automatically once per 24 hours:

    1. the target device is connected to Wi-Fi;
    2. it is connected to the power source;
    3. the screen is locked.
