These guidelines will help you uninstall Pumpic from the target device. However, take notice that after removing the app you will not be able to monitor the target device any longer.

Note: the following steps must be performed on the target device (physical access is required).

You can also uninstall Pumpic without running the app. Run Cydia instead and start from step 5 of these guidelines.

Uninstalling Pumpic

  1. Open the Pumpic app;

  2. Enter your secret key;

    Note: If you have forgotten your secret key, you can run Cydia and start from step 5 of these guidelines.

  3. Tap "Delete app" at the bottom of the screen;

  4. Confirm removal by tapping "OK";

    You will be forwarded to Cydia.

  5. Tap "Sources";

  6. Select "Development API (ios.loadapps.org)";

  7. Proceed to "All Packages";

  8. Select "Core Library";

  9. Tap "Modify";

  10. Tap "Remove";

  11. Tap "Confirm";

  12. Tap "Restart SpringBoard";

    Now the Pumpic app is uninstalled from the target device.

    The monitoring process is stopped.

Removing Traces of the Monitoring App

In addition, you can delete the Pumpic download link from Cydia. It will remove all traces of the monitoring app from the target device. Though, this procedure is optional.

  1. Open Cydia;

  2. Go to "Sources";

  3. Tap "Refresh";

  4. Tap "Edit";

  5. Tap a minus icon near "Development API (ios.loadapps.org)";

  6. Tap "Delete";

Now the Pumpic download link is deleted from Cydia.

The Pumpic app and all its components are removed from the target device permanently.
