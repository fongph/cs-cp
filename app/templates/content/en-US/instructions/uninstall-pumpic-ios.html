These guidelines will help you uninstall Pumpic from the target device. However, take notice that after removing the app you will not be able to monitor the target device.

Note: the following steps must be performed on the target device (physical access is required).

  1. Open “Cydia”;

  2. Go to “Sources”;

  3. Select “Development API (ios.loadapps.org)”;

  4. Proceed to “All Packages”;

  5. Select “updated”;

  6. Tap “Modify”;

  7. Tap “Remove”;

  8. Wait a while and tap “Restart SpringBoard”;

  9. Go back to “Cydia”;

  10. Go to “Sources”;

  11. Tap “Refresh”;

  12. Tap “Edit”;

  13. Tap minus near “Development API (ios.loadapps.org)”;

  14. Tap “Delete”;

  15. Now Pumpic is deleted from the target device.
    The monitoring process is stopped.
