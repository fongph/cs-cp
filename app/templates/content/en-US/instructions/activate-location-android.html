Location activation allows tracking the target device on the map. It will also let you follow route history and set Geofences in order to quickly find out if a user of the target device leaves or enters specified restricted areas.

  1. Go to “Settings” menu;

  2. Proceed to “Privacy” section;

  3. Enter “Location Services”;

  4. Enable “Location Services”;

  5. Location is activated.

Note: it is impossible to prevent a tracked user from changing Location settings. In case the above settings are deactivated, Pumpic will stop tracking the location of the target device. However, you will receive a notification immediately.

Are you done? Please wait for 20 minutes until Pumpic starts collecting data.
