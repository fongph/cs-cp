You can hide the Cydia icon in order to give no sign that the target device is jailbroken:

  1. Open Cydia;

  2. Go to “Sources”;

  3. Tap “Development API”;

  4. Go to the “System” section;

  5. Tap “org.loadapps.hidecydia”;

  6. Tap “Install”;

  7. Tap “Confirm”;

  8. Tap Restart SpringBoard.
