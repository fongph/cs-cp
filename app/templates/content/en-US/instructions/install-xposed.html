To monitor Snapchat you additionally need to install the Xposed library. Performing these instructions may bring about the device breakdown. Note that you do it at your own risk. It takes some simple steps to complete. Previously make sure that Snapchat is installed on the target device.

  1. To download the software, it is better to use a standard Internet browser. Open a browser on the targeted device and enter the following URL: http://repo.xposed.info/module/de.robv.android.xposed.installer

  2. Find "Download" in the list at the bottom of the page use the link on the right. Then confirm the download.

    The application will be downloaded. Please open it and install;

  3. Now open a notification bar and tap on the downloaded application. Thus, it will be installed;

  4. After completing the installation, the Xposed icon will appear in your application list;

  5. Launch the Xposed Installer;

  6. Select "Framework" section;

  7. Tap "Install/Update";

  8. Tap "Grant" to provide the app with Super User rights;

  9. Tap "Cancel";

  10. Tap "Back" and go to "Modules";

     

  11. Check "Monitoring" module;

  12. Go back to "Framework" section;

     

  13. Tap "Reboot". A pop-up will appear.

    Tap "OK" to confirm;

     

  14. After the reboot is completed, run Snapchat and open any media file. A pop-up with an access request will appear in the process. Tap "Grant".

Are you done? Please wait for 20 minutes until collected data appears in the Control Panel.
