To monitor Snapchat you additionally need to install the Xposed library. The process is fast and easy. It takes some simple steps to complete.

  1. To download the software, it is better to use a standard Internet browser. Open a browser on the targeted device and enter the following URL: http://dlxda.xposed.info/modules/de.robv.android.xposed.installer_v32_de4f0d.apk

    After completing the installation, the Xposed icon will appear in your application list.

  2. Launch the Xposed Installer;

  3. Select "Framework" section;

  4. Tap "Install/Update";

  5. Tap "Back" and go to "Modules";

     

  6. Check "Monitoring" module;

  7. Go back to "Framework" section;

     

  8. Tap "Reboot". A pop-up will appear.

    Tap "OK" to confirm.

     

Note: it is impossible to prevent a tracked user from changing Location settings. In case the above settings are deactivated, Pumpic will stop tracking the location of the target device. However, you will receive a notification immediately.

Are you done? Please wait for 20 minutes until Pumpic starts collecting data.
