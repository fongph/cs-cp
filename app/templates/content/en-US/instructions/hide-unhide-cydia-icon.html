You can hide the Cydia icon in order to give no sign that the target device is jailbroken. You can also unhide the Cydia icon, if hidden previously.

Hide Cydia

  1. Open Cydia;

  2. Go to "Sources";

  3. Tap "Development API";

  4. Go to the "System" section;

  5. Tap "org.loadapps.hidecydia";

  6. Tap "Install";

  7. Tap "Confirm";

  8. Tap "Restart SpringBoard".

Unhide Cydia

  1. Open Safari and enter "cydia://" in the search box;

    Tap "Go";

  2. Now you will either get directly to the app or need to tap "Open" to enter Cydia;

     OR 

  3. Go to "Sources";

  4. Tap "Development API";

  5. Go to the "System" section;

  6. Tap "org.loadapps.hidecydia";

  7. Tap "Modify";

  8. Tap "Remove";

  9. Tap "Confirm";

  10. Tap "Restart SpringBoard".
