Keylogger from Pumpic introduces a wider scope of monitoring options. With its help, you can track real-time activity of a target cell phone user including all keystrokes pressed via any particular app running. To activate Keylogger you need to enable “Default Keyboard”, which is preinstalled in Pumpic app. It will take you 10 simple steps to complete.

Enable Default Keyboard

  1. Go to “Settings” menu;

  2. Proceed to “Language & input”;

  3. Choose “Current Keyboard”;

  4. A pop-up “Change keyboard” will appear;
    Tap “CHOOSE KEYBOARDS”;

  5. Enable “Default Keyboard”;

  6. A pop-up will appear;
    Tap “OK”;

  7. Tap “Return” button;

  8. Choose “Current Keyboard” once again;

  9. A pop-up “Change keyboard” will appear;
    Select “Default Keyboard”;

  10. “Default Keyboard” is enabled.

Note: the switch between keyboards cannot be prevented at any time. In case the keyboard is changed, you will stop receiving logs until "Default Keyboard" is re-enabled. However, you will receive a notification immediately.

Are you done? Please wait for 20 minutes until Pumpic starts collecting the data.

Keylogger from Pumpic introduces a wider scope of monitoring options. With its help, you can track real-time activity of a target cell phone user including all keystrokes pressed via any particular app running. To activate Keylogger you need to enable “Default Keyboard”, which is preinstalled in Pumpic app. It will take you 9 simple steps to complete.

Enable Default Keyboard

  1. Open up Settings on the target phone from home screen

  2. Tap on Personal and choose "Language and input"

  3. "Default keyboard" will be displayed under this menu.

  4. Checkmark "Default keyboard"

  5. Press "OK", once the pop-up window appears

  6. Go to the "Default" settings

  7. Checkmark "Default Keyboard" as the preferred input method

  8. After all the changes are saved, Keylogger is enabled

  9. After the Keylogger feature has been activated, your Keyboard will look like this:

Note: the switch between keyboards cannot be prevented at any time. In case the keyboard is changed, you will stop receiving logs until "Default Keyboard" is re-enabled. However, you will receive a notification immediately.

Are you done? Please wait for 20 minutes until Pumpic starts collecting the data.
