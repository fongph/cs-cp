Location activation allows tracking the target device on the map on your request.

iCloud location tracking requires Find my iPhone and Location Services to be enabled. Follow further steps to enable them. If either FMiP or Location Services has been enabled already, then just skip referring steps.

Enable Location Services

  1. Go to “Settings” menu;

  2. Proceed to “Privacy” section;

  3. Enter “Location Services”;

  4. Enable “Location Services”;

  5. Location is activated.

Enable Find my iPhone

  1. Go back to “Settings“;

  2. Go to "Settings", select Apple ID and proceed to "iCloud";

    Note: for iOS 9-10.3.1 the path is Settings > iCloud

  3. Proceed to "Find My iPhone" section;

  4. Enable "Find My iPhone" service;

    A pop-up "Find My iPhone" will appear – tap "Allow" to continue.

     

  5. Turn on "Send Last Location" option;

  6. Now "Find My iPhone" service is activated.

Note: The user of the target device can change Location settings. In case the above settings are deactivated, Pumpic will stop tracking the location of the target device. You will receive a notification immediately. However, we recommend you to lock Location settings: take the target device, open “Settings”, go to General > Restrictions, tap on “Enable Restrictions” and set a password. Then go to Restrictions > Location Services and choose "Don't Allow Changes".

Are you done? Go to the location section and request the current location now.
