These guidelines will help you uninstall Pumpic from the target device. However, take notice that after removing the app you will not be able to monitor the target device.

Note: the following steps must be performed on the target device (physical access is required).

Administrator rights withdrawal

  1. Enter “Settings”;

  2. Go to “Security”

  3. Proceed to “Device administrators”;

  4. Uncheck “Monitoring”;

  5. Tap “Deactivate”.

Uninstallation process after administrator rights withdrawal

  1. Enter “Settings”;

  2. Go to “Apps” or ”Applications”;

  3. Select “Monitoring”;

  4. Tap “Uninstall”;

  5. Confirm uninstallation, tap “OK”;

  6. It may take a while;

  7. Now Pumpic is removed from the target device.
    The monitoring process is stopped.
