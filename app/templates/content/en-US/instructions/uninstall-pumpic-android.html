These guidelines will help you uninstall Pumpic from the target device. However, take notice that after removing the app you will not be able to monitor the target device any longer.

Note: the following steps must be performed on the target device (physical access is required).

There are two ways to delete the Pumpic app from the target device. The first one, in-app removal, is the easiest one. It lets you uninstall Pumpic from inside the app. If you face any difficulties with it, you can delete the app from the device settings menu as well.

In-app Removal

  1. Open the Pumpic app;

  2. Enter your secret key;

  3. Tap "Delete Application" at the bottom of the screen;

  4. Confirm removal by tapping "OK";

  5. Now the Pumpic app is uninstalled from the target device.

The monitoring process is stopped.

Administrator rights withdrawal

  1. Enter "Settings";

  2. Go to "Security"

  3. Proceed to "Device administrators";

  4. Uncheck "Monitoring";

  5. Tap "Deactivate".

Uninstallation process after administrator rights withdrawal

  1. Enter "Settings";

  2. Go to "Apps" or "Applications";

  3. Select "Monitoring";

  4. Tap "Uninstall";

  5. Confirm uninstallation, tap "OK";
