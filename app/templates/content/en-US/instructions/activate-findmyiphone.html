Follow the steps below to activate Find My iPhone service on the target iOS device.

  1. Go to "Settings" menu.

  2. Go to "Settings", select Apple ID and proceed to "iCloud";

    Note: for iOS 9-10.3.1 the path is Settings > iCloud

  3. Proceed to "Find My iPhone" section.

  4. Enable "Find My iPhone" service;

    A pop-up "Find My iPhone" will appear â€“ tap "Allow" to continue.

     

  5. Turn on "Send Last Location" option.

  6. Now "Find My iPhone" service is activated.
