New clients receive an email with a link to CP after they make a purchase. After logging in to CP they navigate right to the installation wizard, where they can set up their devices and start monitoring.

1. Select a Subscription Plan

A client will see this step if only they have more than one subscription. If they have only one subscription, they proceed to step 2.

2. Select a Platform

3. Assign new device

Clients go through the instruction steps to prepare their devices for Pumpic installation.

Existing clients, who used Pumpic before, will see their previously added devices. For example, they have used Pumpic app and their subscription has expired. By tapping on “Assign Subscription” a client will move right to step 5. In case if they have removed application from the target device, they will have to follow reinstallation instructions.

Reinstallation instructions – ссылка на http://cp.pumpic.com/instructions/installing-ios.

4. Activate Device

Clients must enter generated PIN code on their devices within 15 minutes.

5. Complete Installation

Clients have successfully assigned the target devices to their Subscription Plans. Now they can optionally set name to the tracked devices.

Additionally all users will receive an email with the confirmation regarding adding of a new device.
