You can easily track the target iOS device without jailbreak. However, previously make sure that:

  1. To monitor photos on the target device, they must be uploaded to a backup. That’s why you need to disable iCloud Photo Library.

    Go to “Settings” and proceed to “iCloud”;

    Choose “Photos”;

    Disable “iCloud Photo Library”;

     

  2. iCloud Backup is enabled on the target iPhone, iPad or iPod Touch.

    To make sure, take the target Apple iOS device. Then go to Settings > iCloud > Storage & Backup. If iCloud Backup is off, please turn it ON (as shown below).

      

  3. iCloud Backup is turned on for each of the apps you are going to monitor.

    To make sure, go to Settings > iCloud > Storage > Manage Storage, choose your device and then mark all required applications.

    Note that some of applications you are going to monitor may be located under "Show all Apps". Tap "Show all Apps" and mark all required applications there as well.

        

  4. You have Apple ID of the target iOS device and know the password.

  5. Two-step verification is disabled in the account of the target device.

    6. If all of the aforementioned requirements are satisfied, you can proceed to the next step of these guidelines.

    Note: iCloud backs up the data from the target device automatically once per 24 hours. In addition, the following requirements should be also satisfied:

    1. the target device is connected to Wi-Fi;
    2. it is connected to the power source;
    3. the screen is locked.

    It all means that the data from the target devices might be sent to your Control Panel with a day delay. If you need the data updates to be uploaded as soon as possible, you can perform iCloud Backup manually. Go to Settings > iCloud > Storage & Backup (as shown above) and tap Back Up Now.

     

    Wait till backup process is finished. This may take several minutes.
